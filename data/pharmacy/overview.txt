PHARMACY DEPARTMENT OVERVIEW

The Department of Pharmacy at City University Peshawar is committed to producing qualified pharmacists who can serve the healthcare sector. Our program combines pharmaceutical sciences with clinical training to ensure graduates are well-prepared for the pharmaceutical industry and healthcare settings.

The department has state-of-the-art laboratories and strong industry connections for internships and placements.

Programs Offered:
- Pharm-D (5 years Doctor of Pharmacy)

Career Opportunities:
- Community Pharmacy
- Hospital Pharmacy
- Pharmaceutical Industry
- Regulatory Affairs
- Research & Development
- Clinical Pharmacy
