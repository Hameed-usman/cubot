FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Q: What are the admission requirements?
A: Minimum 45% marks in Intermediate for Bachelor's programs. Specific requirements vary by department.

Q: What is the fee structure?
A: Fee structure varies by program. Please contact the admissions office or visit our website for detailed information.

Q: When do admissions open?
A: Fall semester admissions open in June, Spring semester admissions open in December.

Q: Is there an entrance test?
A: Yes, most programs require an entrance test. Check with your chosen department for details.

Q: What facilities are available on campus?
A: Library, computer labs, sports complex, cafeteria, medical services, Wi-Fi, and transport are available.

Q: How can I contact the admissions office?
A: Email: admissions@cityuniversity.edu.pk | Phone: +92-91-1234567

Q: Are there scholarship opportunities?
A: Yes, merit-based and need-based scholarships are available. Contact the student affairs office for details.

Q: What is the medium of instruction?
A: English is the medium of instruction for all programs.

Q: Is there hostel facility?
A: Yes, limited hostel facilities are available for outstation students. Apply early.

Q: How do I apply for transfer?
A: Transfer applications are considered based on seat availability. Contact the admissions office.
