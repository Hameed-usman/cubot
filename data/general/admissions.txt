ADMISSIONS INFORMATION

How to Apply:
1. Obtain the admission form from the university website or admissions office
2. Submit the completed form along with required documents
3. Appear for the entrance test (if required)
4. Attend the interview (if shortlisted)
5. Pay the admission fee to secure your seat

Eligibility Criteria:
- For Bachelor's programs: At least 45% marks in Intermediate or equivalent
- For Master's programs: At least 45% marks in Bachelor's degree
- Specific eligibility varies by department

Required Documents:
- Copy of CNIC/B-Form
- Passport-size photographs
- Educational certificates
- Entrance test result (if applicable)

Important Dates:
- Fall Semester: Applications open in June
- Spring Semester: Applications open in December
