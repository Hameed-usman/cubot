CAMPUS FACILITIES

Library:
The university library has over 50,000 books, journals, and digital resources. It provides quiet study areas, computer labs, and research spaces for students and faculty.

Computer Labs:
Fully equipped computer labs with high-speed internet access and modern software for academic and research purposes.

Sports Complex:
- Indoor and outdoor sports facilities
- Gymnasium
- Cricket and football grounds
- Tennis court
- Basketball court

Cafeteria:
Multiple food outlets providing a variety of cuisines at affordable prices.

Medical Services:
On-campus medical clinic with qualified staff and emergency services.

Transport:
Bus service available for students commuting from various parts of Peshawar.

Parking:
Secure parking facilities for students and staff.

Wi-Fi:
Campus-wide wireless internet connectivity.
