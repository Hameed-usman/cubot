NURSING SEMESTER PLAN

Generic BSN (8 Semesters):
- Semester 1-2: Foundation nursing, basic sciences
- Semester 3-4: Medical-surgical nursing, pharmacology
- Semester 5-6: Specialty nursing (pediatric, maternal, community)
- Semester 7-8: Advanced practice, leadership, clinical practicum

Post-RN BSN (4 Semesters):
- Semester 1-2: Advanced nursing concepts
- Semester 3-4: Leadership, research, practicum

Clinical training begins from Year 1 in affiliated hospitals.
