NURSING DEPARTMENT OVERVIEW

The Department of Nursing at City University Peshawar produces compassionate and skilled nursing professionals. Our program combines classroom learning with extensive clinical practice in hospitals and healthcare settings.

The department is committed to producing nurses who can provide quality healthcare and contribute to the wellbeing of communities.

Programs Offered:
- Generic BSN (4 years Bachelor of Science in Nursing)
- Post-RN BSN (2 years)

Career Opportunities:
- Hospital Nursing
- Community Health Nursing
- Nursing Education
- Healthcare Management
- Specialization in ICU, OT, Emergency
