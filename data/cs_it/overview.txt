CS & IT DEPARTMENT OVERVIEW

The Department of Computer Science and Information Technology at City University Peshawar is one of the most sought-after departments in the region. Established with a vision to produce skilled IT professionals, the department offers cutting-edge programs in computing and information technology.

Our faculty consists of highly qualified professors, industry experts, and researchers who are committed to providing quality education. The department maintains strong ties with the IT industry to ensure our curriculum remains relevant and students are well-prepared for the job market.

Programs Offered:
- BS Computer Science (4 years)
- BS Information Technology (4 years)
- MS Computer Science (2 years)
- MS Information Technology (2 years)

Career Opportunities:
Our graduates work as software developers, web developers, database administrators, network engineers, IT consultants, system analysts, and cybersecurity experts in leading organizations locally and internationally.
