CS & IT SEMESTER PLAN

BS Computer Science (8 Semesters):
Semester 1-2: Foundation courses, programming basics, mathematics
Semester 3-4: Core computer science, data structures, algorithms
Semester 5-6: Advanced topics, databases, networks, software engineering
Semester 7-8: Specialization courses, final year project, internship

BS Information Technology (8 Semesters):
Semester 1-2: IT fundamentals, programming, web basics
Semester 3-4: Database, networks, systems analysis
Semester 5-6: Web development, security, project management
Semester 7-8: Advanced IT applications, project, industrial training

MS Programs (4 Semesters):
Semester 1-2: Coursework
Semester 3-4: Thesis research and writing

Each semester is approximately 16-18 weeks including study break and exams.
