BBA SEMESTER PLAN

BBA (8 Semesters):
- Semester 1-2: Business fundamentals, foundation courses
- Semester 3-4: Core business subjects, analytical skills
- Semester 5-6: Advanced management, specialization tracks
- Semester 7-8: Strategic management, internship, final project

MBA (4 Semesters):
- Semester 1-2: Core business courses
- Semester 3-4: Specialization + Thesis

Each semester includes 16-18 weeks of instruction plus exams.
