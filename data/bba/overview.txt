BBA DEPARTMENT OVERVIEW

The Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA) program at City University Peshawar is designed to produce skilled business professionals and future leaders. Our curriculum combines theoretical knowledge with practical skills to prepare students for the dynamic business environment.

The department emphasizes critical thinking, leadership skills, and entrepreneurial mindset. Students gain hands-on experience through internships, case studies, and business projects.

Programs Offered:
- BBA (4 years)
- BBA (Evening)
- MBA (2 years)

Career Opportunities:
Graduates work as business analysts, marketing managers, human resource managers, financial analysts, entrepreneurs, and operations managers in various industries.
